Definition

The Model represents the application's data and business logic. It manages information, processes data, and communicates with the database.

Responsibilities of the Model

  • Store application data.
  • Manage business logic.
  • Retrieve data from databases.
  • Update and delete records.
  • Provide data to the Controller.

Examples of Data Managed by the Model

  1. User information
  2. Product details
  3. Student records
  4. Employee information
  5. Customer orders
